Getting Started

Introduction

Welcome to Privashhood — the first privacy-first research platform built natively for Robinhood Chain. We give you verified, real-time market insights while keeping every query you make completely private and encrypted.

Unlike traditional research platforms, Privashhood collects zero personal data, logs no queries, and stores nothing about you. Your research stays yours.

🚀 Start Researching

Connect your wallet and start querying the Robinhood Chain ecosystem in minutes. No sign-up required.

🔒 Privacy Guarantee

All queries are encrypted client-side before being sent. We have zero ability to read your research activity.

Quick Start

Get up and running with Privashhood in three simple steps.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Use any EVM-compatible wallet — MetaMask, RH Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect provider. No email or personal info needed.

2

Choose a Research Topic

Type any question about the Robinhood Chain ecosystem — token prices, protocol rankings, gas fees, wallet activity — or pick from suggested queries.

3

Get Verified Results

Receive structured, verified reports in seconds. Every data point is sourced from on-chain data and marked with a verification status.

Privacy Architecture

Privacy is not an afterthought — it's the core design principle. Here's how it works:

🔑

Client-Side Encryption

All queries are encrypted using your wallet's public key before they leave your browser. The server only receives encrypted payloads.

🚫

No Query Logging

Query logs are never stored. Once a query is processed and returned, all traces are purged. We technically cannot reconstruct what you searched.

🌐

Decentralized Data Sources

All market data is pulled from decentralized, on-chain sources — not centralized APIs that could track your requests.

✅

Open Source & Audited

The privacy layer is fully open source and independently audited. You can verify every claim in the codebase yourself.

FAQ

Is Privashhood really private?
Yes. All queries are encrypted client-side before being sent to our servers. We have no technical ability to read or reconstruct your search activity. No query logs are stored anywhere.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Simply connect your EVM wallet and you're ready to go. Your wallet address is used for authentication only and is never linked to your activity on the platform.
When is the $PRVH token launching?
The launch date is TBA. There is no active presale. All official announcements will come from our verified Twitter/X and Telegram channels. Do not trust any third-party claiming to sell $PRVH.
Which wallets are supported?
Any EVM-compatible wallet works: MetaMask, RH Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect-compatible wallets, and hardware wallets like Ledger and Trezor.
Is the code open source?
Yes. The privacy layer and core research engine are fully open source and available on GitHub. Smart contracts will be independently audited before the token launch.
